COP juice

Carrot, Orange, Pear juice

A mix of juices from these fruits are power packed with the goodness of several essential vitamins and minerals such as Vitamin A, C & K, dietary fibre, iron, calcium, etc. This fruit juice for kids not only boasts of essential nutrients but it tastes awesome too.

Ingredients:

  • 2 carrots
  • 1 pear
  • 1 orange

How To Make :

  1. Rinse the fruits and vegetables and cut them in small pieces and put them in your juice
  2. Serve chilled for the best taste.
