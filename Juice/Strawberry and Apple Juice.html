Strawberry and Apple Juice

This juice is one of the favourites for kids. Not only it tastes out of this world, but it is full of vitamin C, dietary fibre, and antioxidants.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of strawberries
  • 1 big apple

How To Make :

  1. Start juicing strawberries first, followed by an apple.
  2. Serve it immediately or with ice.
