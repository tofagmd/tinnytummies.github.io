ABC Juice

Apple, Beetroot and Carrot juice

ABC juice is a rich source of antioxidants, dietary fibre, vitamin C, Iron, Magnesium and Vitamin A.

Ingredients:

  • 1 apple
  • 2 carrots
  • 1/2 beetroot

How To Make :

  1. Wash the fruit and vegetables nicely with clean drinkable water.
  2. Cut the fruits into small pieces or if your juicer allows you to put the whole piece.
  3. Refrigerate the juice before serving it.
  4. Fresh juices taste best when consumed on the same day, but you may store them for up to 2 days
