Kale and Pineapple juice

The green juice for kids is made from kale and pineapple is an excellent source of several Vitamins (A, B1, B2, B3, B6, K, C), fibre, calcium, iron, magnesium, protein and much more. Itâ€™s one of the ideal juices to contribute to the development of your kid.

Ingredients:

  • 200-250g Kale
  • 1-2 large slices of Pineapple

How To Make :

  1. Rinse the fruit and vegetable through fresh drinking water.
  2. Put them either cut or whole (if your juicer allows whole) in the juicer.
  3. Store the juice in the refrigerator for a few minutes before serving it.
