Steak Salad

Nutrition:

Calories – 600 , Protein – 43 g ,Carbohydrates – 30 g ,Fat – 36 g ,Fibre – 3.5 g , Sodium – 520 mg

Ingredients:

  • Onion
  • Pepper
  • Honey
  • Salt
  • Steak
  • Olive oil
  • Mustard
  • Vinegar
  • Potatoes
  • Mixed herbs
  • Cheese
  • Spinach
  • Cherry tomatoes

How To Make :

  1. Boil potatoes in cold salty water for 10 minutes and drain them.
  2. Add oil to pan and season the steak in it with pepper and salt for a few minutes. Keep them aside and slice after they cool.
  3. Add onions to the pan and fry them. Follow with vinegar and honey, coat onions in it and fry till it all disappears. Take onions aside.
  4. Add some more vinegar and honey with mustard in a bowl, followed by olive oil. With some salt and pepper, pour this mixture on potatoes and toss them properly.
  5. In a plate, arrange spinach as a base and place the steak on it, followed by potatoes, tomatoes, cheese, and onions. Sprinkle some mixed herbs and pour the dressing
