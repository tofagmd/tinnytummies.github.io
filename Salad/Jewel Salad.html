Jewel Salad

Nutrition:

Calories – 300,Protein – 16 g,Carbohydrates – 44 g,Fat – 7.5 g,Fibre – 15 g ,And Sodium – 555 mg.

Ingredients:

  • Beans
  • Oranges
  • Pomegranate seeds
  • Vinegar
  • Orange juice
  • Salt
  • Honey
  • Oil
  • Pepper
  • Peas
  • Basil
  • Green onion

How To Make :

  1. Boil a pot of water and add peas to it until they are tender.
  2. In a separate bowl, combine the peas with beans, pomegranates and oranges.
  3. Pour in vinegar, oil, juice, salt, pepper and honey and toss them together. Add this to the bowl and sprinkle some basil on it.
