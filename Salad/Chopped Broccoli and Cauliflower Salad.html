Chopped Broccoli and Cauliflower Salad

Nutrition:

Calories – 180 ,Protein – 5g ,Carbohydrates – 18 g ,Fats – 13 g ,Fibre – 4 g ,Sodium – 215 mg

Ingredients:

  • Broccoli, chopped
  • Cauliflower, chopped
  • Nuts
  • Raisins
  • Black pepper
  • Buttermilk
  • Garlic, chopped
  • Sugar
  • Lemon juice
  • Salt
  • Vinegar
  • Mayonnaise

How To Make :

  1. Run the chopped vegetables in a process until they turn into small pieces.
  2. Take a bowl and mix together vinegar, mayonnaise, lemon juice, garlic and sugar. Add some buttermilk and mix it well. Sprinkle some pepper and salt as well.
  3. Add the vegetables to the bowl and garnish with raisins, some dressing and a few nuts.
