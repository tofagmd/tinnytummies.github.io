Chicken And Mango Salad

Nutrition:

Calories –360 ,Protein –20g ,Carbohydrates –25g ,Fats –25g ,Fibre –3g ,Sodium –344mg

Ingredients:

  • Shredded chicken
  • Coriander
  • Orange juice
  • Lime juice
  • Olive oil
  • Onion
  • Cucumber
  • Mango pieces
  • Lettuce
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Honey

How To Make :

  1. Take a bowl and put together chicken, onion, cucumber, mango and lettuce in it.
  2. In another bowl, add olive oil along with orange juice, lime juice, pepper, sugar and salt and mix it well. Pour this over the salad and toss it together. Garnish with coriander.
