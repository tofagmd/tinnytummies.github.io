Cookie Monster Popcorn

Perfect for the cookie obsessed.

Ingredients:

  • 1 bag microwave popcorn
  • 2 c. white chocolate chips
  • 1 tsp. canola or vegetable oil
  • Blue food coloring
  • 1 1/2 c. mini Chips Ahoy, plus crushed cookies for garnish
  • 1 1/2 c. mini Oreos, plus crushed cookies for garnish
  • Candy eyeballs, for garnish

How To Make :

  1. Pop the popcorn according to package instructions. Set aside.
  2. Combine the white chocolate chips and vegetable oil in a small, microwave-safe bowl and heat in 20-second intervals, stirring in between, until fully melted. Stir in the blue food coloring.
  3. Place the popcorn in a large bowl-being careful to exclude any kernelsâ€”and add about half of the blue chocolate mixture. Stir, adding more chocolate, until the popcorn is coated.
  4. Stir in Chips Ahoy and Oreos. Spread onto a baking sheet in an even layer and top with candy eyeballs and crushed cookies.
  5. Chill in the refrigerator for 20 to 25 minutes before serving.
